## ðŸ—£ï¸ Behavioral Questions

### STAR Method

**S**ituation - Set the context  
**T**ask - Describe your responsibility  
**A**ction - Explain what you did  
**R**esult - Share the outcome

### Common Questions

**Leadership & Teamwork:**
1. Tell me about a time you led a project
2. Describe a conflict with a team member and how you resolved it
3. How do you handle disagreements with your manager?
4. Tell me about a time you helped a struggling team member

**Problem-Solving:**
1. Describe your most challenging technical problem
2. Tell me about a time you failed
3. How do you approach learning new technologies?
4. Describe a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information

**Project Experience:**
1. Walk me through a project you're proud of
2. How do you prioritize tasks?
3. Tell me about a time you missed a deadline
4. Describe a project that didn't go as planned

**Company-Specific:**
1. Why do you want to work here?
2. Why are you leaving your current job?
3. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
4. What's your greatest strength/weakness?

### Preparation Tips
- Prepare 5-7 stories covering different scenarios
- Practice out loud
- Keep answers concise (2-3 minutes)
- Be honest and authentic
- Show growth and learning

## ðŸ¢ Company-Specific Preparation

### FAANG Interview Process

**Google**
- 4-5 technical rounds
- Focus: Algorithms, data structures, system design
- Coding: Google Docs (no IDE)
- Behavioral: Googleyness and leadership

**Meta (Facebook)**
- 2 phone screens + onsite
- Focus: Product sense, coding, system design
- Behavioral: Core values alignment

**Amazon**
- Leadership Principles (14 principles)
- 5-7 rounds including bar raiser
- Focus: Behavioral (50%), technical (50%)

**Netflix**
- Culture fit is critical
- Senior-level focus
- Freedom and responsibility

**Apple**
- Product-focused
- Multiple rounds with different teams
- Attention to detail

### Salary Negotiation

**Research:**
- Use levels.fyi for salary data
- Know your market value
- Consider total compensation (base + bonus + equity)

**Strategy:**
- Never give first number
- Have competing offers
- Negotiate all components
- Be professional and grateful

## üìà Salary Negotiation

### Compensation Structure

**Google:**
- Base: $150K-$300K
- Bonus: 15-30%
- Stock (RSUs): $100K-$500K/year
- Total: $300K-$800K+

**Meta:**
- Base: $150K-$300K
- Bonus: 10-20%
- Stock (RSUs): $150K-$600K/year
- Total: $350K-$900K+

**Amazon:**
- Base: $150K-$185K (capped)
- Bonus: 15-20%
- Stock (RSUs): $100K-$400K/year
- Sign-on: $50K-$200K
- Total: $300K-$700K+

**Apple:**
- Base: $150K-$300K
- Bonus: 10-20%
- Stock (RSUs): $100K-$400K/year
- Total: $300K-$700K+

**Netflix:**
- Base: $300K-$500K+ (cash heavy)
- Bonus: Included in base
- Stock (Options): Varies
- Total: $400K-$800K+

### Negotiation Tips

1. **Get Multiple Offers** - Leverage competition
2. **Know Your Worth** - Use levels.fyi
3. **Negotiate All Components** - Base, bonus, stock, sign-on
4. **Be Professional** - Grateful but firm
5. **Consider Total Comp** - Not just base salary
6. **Location Matters** - SF/NYC pay more
7. **Level Matters** - L4 vs L5 is significant
